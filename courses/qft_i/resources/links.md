## Sidney Coleman's Harvard Lectures

**Legendary** — Coleman taught QFT at Harvard for decades. These 1975-76 lectures influenced generations of physicists.

### Video Lectures (Free)

**Harvard Physics archive**: https://www.physics.harvard.edu/events/videos/Phys253

Alternative sources:
- YouTube playlist: Search "Sidney Coleman QFT lectures"
- Internet Archive: https://archive.org/details/SidneyColemanQuantumFieldTheory

### Lecture Notes (LaTeXed)

The notes from Coleman's course, transcribed and cleaned up:
- **arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/1110.5013

## Textbooks (Physical Copies Recommended)

### Required

1. **Peskin & Schroeder** — *An Introduction to Quantum Field Theory*
   - ISBN: 9780201503975
   - The standard graduate text

2. **Weinberg** — *The Quantum Theory of Fields, Vol. 1: Foundations*
   - ISBN: 9780521670531
   - More foundational/philosophical

### Recommended Supplements

| Book | Strength |
|------|----------|
| Zee, *QFT in a Nutshell* | Intuition, big picture |
| Srednicki, *QFT* | Clear, modern |
| Schwartz, *QFT and Standard Model* | Pedagogical, self-contained |
| Ryder, *Quantum Field Theory* | Good on path integrals |
